═══ GENERAL TRANSIT RULES ═══

RULE_TRANSIT_VEDHA_SHANI | Saturn auspicious in transit: 3rd, 6th, 11th houses from Moon. Corresponding Vedha houses (నిర్వీర్యం): 12th, 9th, 5th respectively. If Sun transits Vedha house simultaneously — Vedha cancelled.

RULE_TRANSIT_GENERAL_1 | If a planet yielding adverse effects is aspected by a benefic, his evil effects are cancelled.
RULE_TRANSIT_GENERAL_2 | A planet loses his capability to do good if he is aspected by an inimical planet.
RULE_TRANSIT_GENERAL_3 | If Sun, Mars, Jupiter and Saturn transit 1st, 8th or 10th from Moon — danger to life, fall from position, loss of wealth.
RULE_TRANSIT_GENERAL_4 | Saturn in 1st from natal Moon causes loss of honour and wealth, danger to life.
RULE_TRANSIT_DASHA_PRIORITY | Classical rule: "దశా ఫలం గోచారేణ సంశోధ్యమ్" — Dasha phala is PRIMARY. Transit modifies but does not replace dasha results. Weight: Dasha 70%, Transit 30%.
